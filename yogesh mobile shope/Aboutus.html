Laptop screen repair involves fixing issues related to the display of your laptop, such as physical damage, flickering, dimming, distorted colors, or a completely non-functional screen. Before attempting any repairs, diagnose the issue with your laptop screen. Determine whether the problem is due to physical damage, software-related issues, or hardware failures. Attempting complex repairs without the proper knowledge and tools can lead to additional problems or void your warranty. If you're not confident in your repair skills, it's best to leave laptop screen repairs to experts who have the experience and expertise to handle the task. Certain software applications or settings can cause screen problems. Boot your laptop into Safe Mode to see if the issue persists.
